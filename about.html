Our Heritage

A Taste of Junnar Tradition

Located conveniently at CS Mall in Bodake Nagar, Junnar, More Veg Restaurant & Misal House was established with one simple, unwavering goal: to serve the most authentic, hygienic, and delicious pure vegetarian food to our local community and travelers alike.

Junnar is a city steeped in profound historical significance, nestled at the base of the great Shivneri Fort—the esteemed birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. We take immense pride in being part of this vibrant cultural tapestry.
